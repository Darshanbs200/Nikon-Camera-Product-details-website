    REVIEWS

    First Impressions - I am extremely happy with the investment I made. I have been using this camera for a week now in various scenarios such as wildlife, portrait and product shoots. I am extremely happy with the quality of the output and the keeper rate. The camera feels amazing in hands and there is huge improvement in terms of autofocus. To shoot at 20 FPS was an absolute delight. Video options are amazing and the file size is less heavy than my expectations. This is just initial impression and I would repost a review after using this camera extensively. 120 FPS view finder is an absolute delight AOI - The camera is packed with features and it becomes challenging to keep up with the items added to the menu. It will take a little bit of learning curve for the users moving up from Z6 or Z7 series. Auto focus struggled a little when it came to birds. This might be more of a user error than the issue with the camera. More to come on this after few weeks. Features like dynamic zoom and skin smoothening are not applicable in all modes. The camera has alot of offer and it would be great if Nikon can fix this using firmware Wishlist - Upgrade view finder resolution and the resolution of the touchscreen at the back of the camera (Though I wont be able to get the benefit of it as I have already invested in this body) Overall thoughts - The camera gives more value than you expect Recommends this product ✔ Yes
